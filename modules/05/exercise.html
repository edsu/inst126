Module 5 Exercise

Rewrite the pay calculation from the previous exercise by creating a function called compute_pay that will take two arguments: hours and rate and will return the pay. Use the function to compute the pay for the hours and rate that were input and print it out.

Bonus:

For an extra credit point see if you can calculate overtime pay of time-and-a-half when someone has worked over 40 hours. So for example if an employee worked 50 hours at 15.00 an hour they would be paid $825.00.

40 * 15 + 10 * (15 + 7.50) = 825
